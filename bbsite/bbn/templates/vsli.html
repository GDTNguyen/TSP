VSLI Data Sets

A VSLI data set is a Very-large-scale integration. This is the study of how to position nodes in a integrated circuit board.102 TSP VLSI data sets studied at the Forschungsinstitut für Diskrete Mathematik, Universität Bonn. The instances in the VLSI collection range in size from 131 nodes to 744,710 nodes. In these puzzels, the cost of travel between cities is specified by the Eulidean distance rounded to the nearest whole number. We took some VSLI problems and ran the Backbone algorithm and nearest_neighbor on them with the goal of getting better results than the heuristic used in the Backbone algorithm.
